
# **Kickstarter Funding Performance (2010–2011)**

## **Overview**

This project analyzes Kickstarter campaigns from 2010–2011 to understand funding trends, category performance and overall campaign outcomes.
The goal is to explore which types of projects perform best and how funding levels changed over time.

---

## **Dataset**

* Total projects: **24,009**
* Time period: **2010–2011**
* Includes: project category, target, pledge, status, backers, and quarterly timelines

---

## **Dashboard Features**

* **Total Projects, Average Target, Average Raised, Average Backers, Success Rate**
* **Project Funding Status** (Fully Funded, Not Fully Funded, Over-Funded)
* **Project Count per Category**
* **Average Amount Raised Over Time (2010 Q1 – 2011 Q4)**
* **Average Amount Raised per Category**
* **Total Amount Raised per Category**
* **Funding Level Segments** (Low, Medium-Low, Medium-High, High)

---

## **Key Findings**

* **Film & Video** and **Music** categories have the most projects and highest overall funding.
* A Huge percentage of projects become **over-funded** (54%).
* Success rate is **56.51%**.
* Average raised amount shows **steady growth** from 2010 to late 2011.
* **Technology**, **Design** showed significant increase over this period.
